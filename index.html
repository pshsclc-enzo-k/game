Aquavaganza

Hello! Welcome to Aquavaganza! This is a game made with CSS, HTML, and Javascript.

You are currently playing on Easy difficulty.

HOW TO PLAY?

Remember that one dinosaur game on Google Chrome that you can play when offline? Well, this is identical to that game!

Press the spacebar on your keyboard to jump. Everytime you successfuly jump over a cactus, you get one point.

The game can only end when the player touches the cactus, or, in easier terms, loses.
